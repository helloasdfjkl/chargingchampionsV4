Who are We?

Charging Champions is a high school robotics team based in Irvine, California that has participated in FIRST competitions since 2012, with two years in FLL, two years in FTC, and three years of FRC. We have competed in regional and super regional competitions, Legoland invitationals, and the World Championships across each competition circuit, and have proven to punch far above our modest means. We do not only compete in FIRST competitions; we have extensive community outreach and STEM initiatives across the world, from California to Canada to Africa, forming FIRST teams and spreading the importance of STEM education to underprivileged areas.
