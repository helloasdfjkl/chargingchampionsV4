Sponsors

Innovation and technology advances rapidly and is essential for economic development. The Charging Champions Robotics Team is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization dedicated to the advancement of our youth in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math) fields. With your sponsorship, you not only contribute to the skills of our youth but also the technological and economic development in our community. With your support, our youth gain the science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence and communication to lead our community into the highest standards for the future.
